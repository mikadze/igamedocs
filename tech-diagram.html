Key Architectural Decisions
1
Bun.js for hot path ‚Äî Native TypeScript runtime with built-in WebSocket support, fast startup times, and unified codebase across all layers.
2
NATS JetStream ‚Äî Purpose-built for multi-channel social topology (Crews, tournaments, Copy-Bet). Redis alone can't partition per-crew at scale.
3
Adaptive rendering ‚Äî Auto-detect device capability: 3D (high-end) ‚Üí 2.5D (mid) ‚Üí 2D sprite (low). Win on $120 phones.
4
Feature flags per jurisdiction ‚Äî Single codebase, infinite market configs. Ship Copy-Bet in Cura√ßao while in GLI review for MGA.

    P->>FE: Place Bet

    NATS->>BUN: Process bet
    BUN->>PG: Record bet
    BUN->>NATS: Confirm bet
    NATS->>WS: Broadcast to room
    WS->>FE: bet.confirmed
    FE->>P: Show bet placed

    Note over BUN: Round starts, multiplier ticks

    loop Every 16ms
        BUN->>NATS: multiplier.tick
        NATS->>WS: Broadcast
        WS->>FE: Update display
    end

    P->>FE: Cash Out
    FE->>WS: cashout.request
    WS->>BUN: Validate & process
    BUN->>PG: Record win
    BUN->>OP: Wallet credit (async)
    BUN->>NATS: cashout.confirmed
    NATS->>WS: Broadcast
    WS->>FE: Show win
